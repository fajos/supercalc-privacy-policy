Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

🔒 Our Commitment to Privacy

SuperCalc is designed with your privacy as a fundamental principle, not an afterthought. We believe you should be able to use a calculator without worrying about who might be watching or what happens to your data.

🛡️ Your calculations are yours alone. They never leave your device.

📋 Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data.

  • All calculations are performed entirely on your device
  • No equations, inputs, or results ever leave your phone
  • We don't use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind
  • We don't have servers to send data to, even if we wanted to
  • No account creation or login required
  • No internet permission requested by the app

🔗 Third-Party Services

SuperCalc does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. The app is completely self-contained and operates independently on your device.

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics platforms
  • No cloud services
  • No social media integrations

👶 Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data from anyone, our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. The app contains only educational mathematical content with no user tracking or data collection mechanisms.

🔐 Data Security

The best data security is not having your data in the first place. Since SuperCalc processes everything locally and never transmits any information, there is no risk of data breaches, leaks, or unauthorized access to your calculations.

💡 Your privacy is guaranteed by design, not by policy.

📝 Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes (for example, if we add features in future updates), we will:

  • Notify you through an app update
  • Post the updated policy on this page
  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top

We recommend checking this page occasionally for any changes.
