# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2025**

## Our Commitment to Privacy

SuperCalc is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe you should be able to use a calculator without worrying about your data.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data.**

- All calculations are performed entirely on your device
- No equations, inputs, or results ever leave your phone
- We don't use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind
- We don't have servers to send data to, even if we wanted to

## Third-Party Services

SuperCalc does not integrate with any third-party services. The app is completely self-contained.

## Children's Privacy

Since we collect no data from anyone, our app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes (for example, if we add features in the future), we will notify you through an app update and post the new policy here.
